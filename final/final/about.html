About This Project

The Entebbe Street Food Guide is designed to celebrate and promote the vibrant street food culture of Entebbe, Uganda. Our mission is to provide locals and tourists with reliable, up-to-date information about where to find delicious, affordable, and hygienic street food.

This website was created as a final project for a web development course, demonstrating responsive design, accessibility, and dynamic content powered by JavaScript.

Our Vision

We believe food is a universal language that brings people together. By connecting food lovers with trusted vendors, we aim to support small businesses and enrich the cultural experience of visitors to Entebbe.

Technology Used

  • HTML5 with semantic markup
  • CSS3 with responsive layout techniques (Grid and Flexbox)
  • JavaScript ES Modules for dynamic data fetching and user interaction
  • Fetch API with asynchronous programming and error handling
  • LocalStorage for persistent user preferences
  • Accessible modal dialogs for vendor details
