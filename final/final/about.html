About Us

Welcome to the Entebbe Street Food Guide! Our mission is to celebrate the vibrant street food culture in Entebbe, Uganda by sharing detailed vendor profiles, delicious recipes, and tips to enjoy street food safely.

Whether you’re a local or a tourist, our guide helps you explore authentic flavors and discover hidden culinary gems.
