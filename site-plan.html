Site Plan – Entebbe Street Food Guide

Site Name

Entebbe Street Food Guide

This name was chosen because it clearly describes the purpose of the website—helping locals and tourists discover and enjoy the diverse street food culture of Entebbe, Uganda.

Optional domain availability: entebbeflavors.com

Site Purpose

The website provides visitors with curated information about the best street food spots in Entebbe. This includes vendor profiles, popular dishes, price ranges, hygiene ratings, and customer reviews. It will also include tips for safe eating and interactive maps to guide exploration.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find affordable and hygienic street food in Entebbe?
  • What are the must-try local dishes when visiting Entebbe?

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: #d84315 – used for headers, buttons, and highlights.
  • Secondary Color: #fbc02d – used for accents, background highlights, and callouts.

Typography
