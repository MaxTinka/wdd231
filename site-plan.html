Site Plan: Entebbe Street Food Guide

Site Name

Entebbe Street Food Guide

This name was chosen because it clearly describes the purpose of the website — helping locals and tourists discover and enjoy the diverse street food culture of Entebbe, Uganda.

Optional domain availability: entebbeflavors.com

Site Purpose

The website provides visitors with curated information about the best street food spots in Entebbe, including vendor profiles, popular dishes, price ranges, hygiene ratings, and customer reviews. It will also include tips for safe eating and interactive maps to guide exploration.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find affordable and hygienic street food in Entebbe?
  • What are the must-try local dishes when visiting Entebbe?

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: #d84315 (deep orange) — used for headers, buttons, and highlights
  • Secondary Color: #fbc02d (warm yellow) — used for accents, background highlights, and callouts

Typography

  • Headings: Roboto Slab (serif) — provides a bold, modern look
  • Body Text: Open Sans (sans-serif) — clean and easy to read
